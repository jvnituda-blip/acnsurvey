VISION

Inspired by the fma charism of joy and simplicity, ACN envisions a Christ-centered Education community holistically forming global citizens responsible to the needs of the Church and society

MISSION

To provide quality and inclusive holistic formation for students, faculty, staff, and collaborators for the nuturance of conscience and for the development of character and competence;
To strive to live the gospel values, guided by examples of Mary, by manifesting the love of God to every person, more particularly the poor and the needy;
To promote responsible stewardship of resources to ensure institutional viability, sustainability, and growth, and
To build and strengthen partnership with local, national and international organization for mobilization of resources and for mutual benefit and growth.

ORATIO IMPERATA
(Prayer for Deliverance from Calamities)
Almighty Father,
We raise our hearts to You in gratitude,
for the wonders of of creation of which we are part,
for Your providence in sustaining us in our needs, and for Your wisdom that guides the course of the universe

We acknowledge our sins against You and the rest of Your creation.

We have not been good stewards of nature. We have confused Your command to subdue the earth.

The environment is made to suffer our wrongdoing, and now we reap the harvest of our abuse and indifference. 		Global warming is upon us.
Typhoons, floods, volcanic eruption, and other naural calamaties occur in the increasing number and intensity.

We turn to you, our loving Father, and beg forgiveness for our sins.
We ask that we, loved ones and our hard earn possessions be spared from the threat of calamaties, natural and man-made.

We beseech You to inspire us all to grow into repsonsible stewards of Your creation, and generous neighbors to those in need. Amen.
